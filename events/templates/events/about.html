General Information

The National Honor Society is a nation-wide community service program designed for high school students. The Okemos chapter is set up to provide Okemos High School Juniors and Seniors with service opportunities that will improve the school and the community and, hopefully, the student as well. Members of N.H.S. are selected based on four criteria: scholarship, character, leadership and service.
